# My View on AI

OK I might upset a few people here, but if you mean AI that creates images, helps you with writing and 
all this then I am fine with it, "AS LONG AS" you are not making money from it and saying the art work
is yours.

**Using AI in my eyes you**:
- Don't own the Art, no one does since it was trained on so many peoples work
- You shouldn't be making money from it as you didn't make it, train it nor put the time
in that people would of to draw something like it.
- Pay large companies money to create AI images, paying for AI images is someone still making
money from learnt work, I saying learnt work not stolen because you can't say someone stole a pixel
of your work as AI uses 1000's of images if not more and only a few will be yours. when creating that image.
- Don't copy out-right someone's style.

That is my view on AI I will use it in my day to day for a bit of fun, I use it for avatars when I get
board with the one I have, but none of my work that i create because I take ownership of my work will
use AI, only on maybe spelling mistakes and making sure it sounds OK never on my artwork and mods.
